## Objective

Elevate the reviewers section in the detail pane to mirror GitHub's upper-right "Reviewers" panel. Move it above the description, add clear status icons per reviewer state, and present it as a prominent, easy-to-scan list.

## Background

The detail pane currently renders reviewers after the description in a minimal `login + state` format (e.g. `octocat              APPROVED`). On GitHub, the reviewers panel sits prominently in the sidebar alongside metadata. This task reorders the detail pane sections and enhances the reviewer display so users can instantly see who has reviewed and what their status is.
